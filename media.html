Stellar Variability Mitigation in Exoplanet Atmospheric Studies

Yaptangco, D., et al.

In preparation for The Astrophysical Journal

This paper presents novel methods for reducing the impact of stellar variability on exoplanet atmospheric characterization using advanced Gaussian Process models and stellar activity indicators.

Exoplanets Stellar Variability Atmospheric Studies

Transit Injection and Recovery Analysis for Exoplanet Detection

Yaptangco, D., Smith, J., Johnson, K.

Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society, submitted

A comprehensive study of exoplanet detectability using transit injection and recovery techniques, with applications to TESS and future space missions.
