Mentorship & Outreach

Inspiring the next generation of astronomers and scientists

My Mentorship Philosophy

I believe that mentorship is one of the most important aspects of scientific research. As someone who has benefited tremendously from the guidance of incredible mentors throughout my journey, I am passionate about paying it forward and supporting the next generation of astronomers, particularly those from underrepresented backgrounds.

My approach to mentorship emphasizes inclusive guidance, hands-on learning, and fostering independence. I strive to create an environment where students feel supported to take risks, ask questions, and develop their own scientific voice.
