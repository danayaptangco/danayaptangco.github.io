Mentorship

Thanks to the positive experiences I’ve had with mentorship, I feel that it’s important to share my experiences and to support the people who are where I was.

This is why I co-founded the Women’s Astronomy & Astrophysics Mentorship Program with Isabella Macias at the University of Florida, and why I continue to seek out opportunities to mentor students.
